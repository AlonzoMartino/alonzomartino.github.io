Being self-conscious of my art and demeaning my work is a problem I go through on a daily basis. Having ill intentions to your own work is the worst thing you can do to yourself. Never degrade yourself. You don’t have to be cocky, but know that every minute of work you put in is a step towards improving.

Compliment yourself and love yourself. If you can’t do that, then how are you supposed to love your own art?
