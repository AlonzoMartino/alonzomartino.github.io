As an upcoming artist, I want to help my fellow peers by sharing my own tips and lessons that I’ve learned from making my own personal works. BY NO MEANS AM I RIGHT OR WRONG, but these are just my own opinions and teachings that I take into account when doing my own work. This website may not go over all of the terms, but hopefully it will help you understand the artist’s journey and how to improve and love your artwork.
