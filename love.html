Doing What You Love

Always do what you love. Down along the path, I feel a lot of my fellow peers forget what they even came to school to begin with. Always be yourself and draw what you love, even if its fanfiction, furry art, or character shipping from shows, or even shipping your own characters. School is important, but loving what you do and making time to do the things you love should be your priority. Incorporating what you learn into your own personal works will only benefit you and make all the stress of going to school worthwhile. Never stop loving the things that make you happy.
